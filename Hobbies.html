My Hobbies



Cooking

I love cooking. from bakeries, desserts, cakes, and cheesecakes to Mexican, Italian, and Turkish cuisine. I'm still on the lookout for new recipes. My favorites are Turkish and Italian cuisine.

Mixology

When I was in college, I also worked as a barback. I picked up a few things and have improved ever since. I can make well-known cocktails and have some of my own.

Reading

Knowledge comes from reading. Without reading, we get nowhere. I enjoy reading fantasy, thrillers, self-help, political, and psychological books. My personal favorites are:

A Tale of Two Cities by Charles Dickens

The Matrix and Philosophy by William Irwin

Lord of the Rings by JRR Tolkein

Last Wish by Andrzej Sapkowski

Gym & Exercise

"A healthy mind in a healthy body." -Mustafa Kemal Atatürk. This quote is my mindset. If you want a healthy mind, first you need a healthy body. Home exercise, workouts, and 10-kilometer walks are part of my weekly routine.

Traveling

Traveling is about gaining new perspectives, breaking away from routines, meeting and learning from new people, and taking a small break from everything that is going on around you.

Being A Handyman

I enjoy both fixing things and creating new things. I do my own electrical, plumbing, and other repairs. When you fix something and see that it works better than before it was broken, that is priceless.

Video Gaming

I've been playing video games since 386 CPUs were available. A turn-based or real-time strategy or games like Mass Effect, which have top-notch stories to play, are my favorites. I also like to play online games with friends. I play video games if I have free time from work and other hobbies.

Hangout with Friends

Hanging out with friends is the best part of my life. Pubs, movies, concerts, video games, travel, gourmet hunting... spending time with friends is priceless. "Whatever you do in this life, it's not legendary unless your friends are there to see it," said Barney (Neil Patrick Harris) on the HIMYM show

Meditation

Three things: awareness, deep relaxation, and mindfulness. My life has changed since I started. It takes my mind off the past and my concerns about the future. Makes me focus on today. Now meditation is a part of my life.
